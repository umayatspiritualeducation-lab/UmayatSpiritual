Who We Are

Our Mission & Sacred Purpose

Umayat Spiritual Education Circle for Self Realization is an Aboriginal Indigenous and Kemetic spiritual education organization rooted in the ancient knowing that was carried across generations — through the bloodlines of the Maroon people of Jamaica, the TasKiGi Creek Nation, and the sacred traditions of Kemet (ancient Egypt). We exist to restore, transmit, and activate that knowing in all who seek it.

Our name, Umayat, holds the frequency of the circle — the unbroken covenant between the ancestors and those walking today. We are not creating something new. We are remembering what was never lost, only buried.

𓋹 The Ankh Principle

Life, breath, and eternal return. We teach that the body is sacred ground and that self-realization begins with remembering who you are before the world named you.

𓂀 The Eye of Knowing

The Wadjet eye — clarity, protection, and restored vision. We restore the inner sight of communities whose ancestral wisdom has been suppressed, colonized, or forgotten.

𓆣 The Kheper Path

Transformation and self-creation. The scarab becomes — it does not wait. We hold space for radical personal becoming rooted in ancient Kemetic cosmology.

𓌀 The Aboriginal Root

Our foundation is Aboriginal Indigenous sovereignty — the knowing that the land, the body, and the cosmos are one. We honor the unbroken line of Indigenous healers and shamans.

I

Self Realization is the birthright of every being. It cannot be granted by an institution or revoked by a system. It lives in you and we teach you to find it.

II

Ancestral Knowing is not history — it is living intelligence. The Revelations from our Ancestors Past and Future flow through this circle in every teaching.

III

The Circle holds all. Indigenous Shaman, Womb Healer, Plant Medicine Therapist, Sacred Scholar — all paths converge here in the ancient covenant of Umayat.

IV

Liberation is the purpose of all true education. We teach not to inform the mind but to free the spirit from every chain — spiritual, psychological, ancestral, and systemic.

"The purpose of all human life is the attainment of the Ancient of Days — to become so aligned with the Source that you move as it moves, breathe as it breathes, and know as it knows."
